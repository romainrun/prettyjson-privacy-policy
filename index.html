Privacy Policy for JSON Viewer & Editor

Last updated: January 2025

This application (“JSON Viewer & Editor”) is developed and published by an independent developer. This privacy policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Data Collection

The app does not collect or store any personal data from users. All JSON content created or edited within the app stays on your device.

2. Advertising

The free version of the app displays non-intrusive banner and rewarded ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data such as device identifiers and ad interactions in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

3. Permissions

The app may request access to files on your device in order to import or export JSON files. No data is transmitted or shared externally.

4. Internet Access

Internet permission is required only for loading JSON from URLs and displaying ads.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.

7. Contact
